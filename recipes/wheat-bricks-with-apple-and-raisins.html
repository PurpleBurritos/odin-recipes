Wheat bricks with quick stew apples

This simple dish is the perfect breakfast it is healthy delicious and very easy.

Ingredients

  • Wheat bricks
  • Oat milk (Or any other plant milk)
  • Apple
  • Raisins
  • Vegan yogurt
  • Mixed seeds and nuts
  • Cinnamon

Method

  1. Put dash of water and cinnamon in a pan and bring it to a boil
  2. As the water is boiled peel and cut an apple and add the apple pieces
  3. Cook until the apple is soft and the water has evaporated
  4. Put 2 wheat bricks in a bowl add your milk, yogurt, mixed seeds and nuts, and the apple slow stewed apples in that order
