Tomato toast

This simple tomato toast has a nice warming spice and a perfect peppery flavour from the rocket this is sure to be your new favorite winter breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Tomato
  • rocket
  • Chilli seasoning

Method

  1. Chop up a tomato
  2. Begin cooking the toast
  3. Cut tomato and begin cooking
  4. Remove the toast when cooked top with tomato and Chilli seasoning
