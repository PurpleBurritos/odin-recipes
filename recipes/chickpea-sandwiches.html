Chickpea sandwiches

This dinner is easy and delicious with a nice kick of mustard.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Chickpeas
  • 1 cup of diced celery
  • 4 table spoons of mayo
  • 1 table spoon of lemon juice
  • 1 table spoon dill
  • 1 table spoon mustard
  • 1 tea spoon garlic powder
  • 1 tea spoon onion powder
  • Bread

Method

  1. Drain chickpeas and cook if necessary
  2. Cool and mush the chickpeas
  3. Finely chop the celery and add to the chickpeas
  4. Add mayo, mustard, lemon juice, herbs and add the spices to the mixture and then combine
  5. then put it in the bread
